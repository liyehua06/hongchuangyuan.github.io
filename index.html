About Us

Our Story

We are a specialized manufacturer and exporter focused on environmentally friendly, realistic artificial flowers and green plants. With our 2,500-square-meter workshop located in Zhaoqing, we have a complete production line and handle all production processes from raw materials to finished products.

With a strong and stable supply chain, we can maintain good delivery times for our customers. Additionally, we support ODM/OEM custom solutions as well as small-batch flexible customization, providing one-stop services from flower design to packaging printing.
